Instructions

Eat food to grow and score points without hitting yourself or the walls.
Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move.
Food: 1.Carrot: Adds 1 point and 1 unit of length.
2.Pumpkin Pie (25%): Adds 3 points and 3 units of length.
3.Sweet Berries (10%): Boost. Double speed for 10 seconds.
4.Golden Apple (5%): Immunity. 10 seconds of invulnerability (pass through walls and your own body).
Timers: Monitor the top corners for active Boost or Immunity durations.
Game Over: Occurs upon wall or self-collision unless the Immunity effect is active.
